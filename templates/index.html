About the Book

A journey through what lies beneath the surface.

Hidden Frequencies explores the deeper currents moving through human life: mythology, aliens, religion, consumption, and evolution. It is not written like a safe explanation. It is written like a signal — one that asks the reader to look again at the systems, beliefs, and patterns shaping the modern world.

Through experience, reflection, and research, this work challenges ordinary assumptions and invites readers into a darker, broader, and more revealing perspective on reality.

Author

David Wright

David Wright writes from lived experience, observation, and the search for what is hidden beneath accepted reality. His work is driven by the belief that modern life is full of signals many people are trained not to see.

Hidden Frequencies is a confrontation with those signals — and an invitation to question what has been normalized.
